Name: Pegasus XL | Ionospheric Connection Explorer (ICON)

Date: October 26, 2018 08:05:00 UTC

Location: Cape Canaveral

Description: 
 Ionospheric Connection Explorer (ICON) is a NASA mission to study Earth's 
thermosphere and ionosphere and the connection between space weather and weather on our planet. 
Mission is planned to last for two years.
