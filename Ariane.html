Ariane 5 ECA | BepiColombo


Date: October 20, 2018 01:45:28 UTC


Location: Ariane Launch Area 3, Kourou

Description: 
BepiColombo is a joint mission of the ESA and the JAXA to the planet Mercury.
The mission comprises two satellites to be launched together:
the Mercury Planetary Orbiter (MPO) and the Mercury Magnetospheric Orbiter (MMO).
The mission will perform a comprehensive study of Mercury, including its magnetic field,
magnetosphere, interior structure and surface.
